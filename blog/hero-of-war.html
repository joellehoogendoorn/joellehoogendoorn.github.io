Master of Fine Arts stories, works in progress, sketches and philosophy. You'll find it all on my blog.

About

I’m a 22-year old aspiring artist who loves spending as much time possible with a painting brush in my hand. Currently studying Fine Art Painting at the Royal Academy of Fine Arts in Antwerp, Belgium.

Works

My main focus is working with subjects that intrigue me and process these subjects in a personal way in my art. The thread in my work is expressing myself and displaying feelings. Music is an very important aspect in my paintings.

I get inspired by travelling, every kind of media, cultures and black holes in society.

Joëlle Hoogendoorn
